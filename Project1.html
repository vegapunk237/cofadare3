Long Term Projects

Sustainable initiatives designed to create lasting change in the fight against drug abuse. These comprehensive programs build strong foundations for prevention, treatment, and recovery.

Our long-term projects represent our commitment to sustainable change. These programs are designed to create infrastructure, systems, and networks that will continue to serve communities for years to come. Through strategic planning, collaborative partnerships, and evidence-based approaches, we're building a future where comprehensive support for addiction prevention and recovery is accessible to all.

Our Long Term Programs

Building sustainable solutions for lasting impact in communities worldwide.

PREVENTION & EDUCATION

Prevention and Education

Building and implementing long-term education and prevention programs in schools and communities to prevent drug use and promote healthy lifestyles. This comprehensive approach includes curriculum development, teacher training, and community engagement strategies that create a culture of prevention.

Learn More
PEER SUPPORT

Peer Support Network

Create networks of peer support groups and mentors to provide ongoing support and guidance. These sustainable networks connect individuals in recovery with experienced mentors, creating communities of hope and mutual support that extend across regions and continue to grow organically.

Learn More
COMMUNITY PROGRAMS

Community Based Programs

Develop programs providing ongoing support and services for individuals in recovery, including counseling, job training, and housing assistance. These holistic programs address the multiple needs of recovery, ensuring individuals have access to comprehensive support throughout their journey.

Learn More
TREATMENT CENTERS

Comprehensive Treatment Centers

Establish centers offering integrated treatment services, including medical, psychological, and social support. These state-of-the-art facilities provide the full spectrum of care needed for effective treatment and long-term recovery, all under one roof.

Learn More
POLICY & ADVOCACY

Policy Development and Advocacy

Engage in long-term advocacy efforts to inform and shape policy and legislation related to addiction treatment and support. Working with governments, international organizations, and civil society, we advocate for evidence-based policies that improve treatment access and reduce stigma.

Learn More
RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

Research and Development

Conduct ongoing research to advance understanding of addiction and develop innovative treatment approaches. Our research initiatives contribute to the global knowledge base while ensuring our programs remain at the cutting edge of evidence-based practice.

Learn More
RECOVERY CENTERS

Recovery Community Centers

Establish centers providing ongoing support and services for individuals in recovery, including social activities, counseling and job training. These welcoming spaces serve as hubs for recovery communities, offering a safe environment for growth, learning, and connection.

Learn More
HARM REDUCTION

Harm Reduction Services

Establish long-term harm reduction services, such as needle exchange programs and methadone clinics, to reduce the risk of overdose and disease transmission. These evidence-based services save lives while connecting individuals to treatment and support services.

Learn More
EMPLOYMENT

Employment and Job Training

Develop programs providing job training, vocational education, and employment opportunities for individuals in recovery. By partnering with employers and training institutions, we help individuals build careers and achieve economic independence essential for sustained recovery.

Learn More
PARTNERSHIPS

Collaboration and Partnership

Establish long-term expansive collaboration and partnerships with other organizations, institutions and countries. Through strategic alliances with governments, NGOs, healthcare systems, and international bodies, we multiply our impact and create coordinated global responses to drug abuse.

Learn More

Building a Sustainable Future

Our long-term projects create lasting infrastructure and systems that will serve communities for generations. Every contribution helps us build treatment centers, train professionals, and establish sustainable programs that transform lives.
