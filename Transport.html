Transport & Drug Abuse

Drug abuse in the transport sector poses serious risks to public safety. Impaired drivers and operators endanger themselves and others. COFADARE works to implement testing mechanisms, education programs, and support services across all transport modalities to ensure safer journeys for everyone.

  • Mandatory Health Screenings
  • Safety Training and Education

Key Areas Affected by Drug Abuse

Understanding the physical and behavioral impacts of drug abuse on transport operators

Central Nervous System (CNS)

Drugs can affect the CNS, leading to impaired cognitive function, judgment, and physical abilities crucial for safe operation of vehicles.

Respiratory System

Certain drugs such as cocaine and inhalants can cause significant damage to the respiratory system, affecting stamina and alertness.

Behavioral Changes

Substance abuse can lead to altered behavior, including aggression, agitation, and impaired decision-making, all critical safety concerns.
