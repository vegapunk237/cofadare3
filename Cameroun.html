History of COFADARE

Understanding our roots and mission in the global fight against drug abuse

Our Global Presence

COFADARE, the Commission for Fight Against Drug Abuse and Rehabilitation, is an International non-governmental Organization whose main mission is to fight against drugs and non-substances abuse and rehabilitation of drug victims back into society. Currently, we are hosted in more than 20 countries around the world, making a global impact in the fight against addiction.

A Global Initiative

This movement is a global initiative and action promoted by a group of experts from diverse fields including Health, Education, Transport, Civil Society, Sport, and more. Today, this initiative continuously integrates many societies, other institutions, and government corporations to fight against this pandemic.

SDG Alignment

Our work aligns with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Article No. 03: Good Health and Well-being. We are committed to ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at all ages, with a specific focus on substance abuse prevention and treatment.

The Challenge We Address

These actions establish a sustainable mechanism to prevent and suppress substances and non-substances abuse which has highly scoped an impact of spheres for the past centuries with more than 1 billion vulnerable persons worldwide. Our comprehensive approach ensures that we address this crisis from all angles.

Our Vision

A world free from drug abuse, where individuals and communities thrive, and those affected by substance use disorders receive comprehensive support and care.

Mission of Cameroon Branch Board

The Cameroon Branch ensures proper implementation of organizational policies in collaboration with the Cameroon government.

Prevention

Education and raising awareness about the risks and consequences of drug abuse through comprehensive community programs and school initiatives

Treatment

Providing accessible, evidence-based treatment and rehabilitation services for individuals struggling with substance use disorders

Support

Offering support and resources for individuals, families, and communities affected by drug abuse, ensuring no one faces this challenge alone
