Support and Management Phase

Comprehensive approach addressing physical, emotional and social needs

Support and Management of Cases

Our support and management phase involves a comprehensive approach that addresses the physical, emotional, and social needs of individuals affected by drug addiction. We operate in different zones based on the level of addiction and intervention required.

ORANGE ZONE

Target Group: People affected and already into drug addiction

This zone focuses on individuals who have begun their journey into drug addiction and require immediate intervention to prevent further deterioration. Our approach includes early detection, counseling, and support mechanisms to help them regain control of their lives.

RED ZONE

Target Group: People highly dependent on drugs with health at high risk

The red zone addresses critical cases where individuals are totally dependent on drugs and their health is at severe risk. This requires intensive medical intervention, psychiatric care, and continuous monitoring to ensure their safety and recovery.

Treatment Mechanism

Our comprehensive treatment approach combines medical expertise, psychological support, and moral guidance to ensure holistic recovery.

Medical Care and Attention

Our medical system involves comprehensive healthcare protocols designed specifically for addiction treatment.

  • Key Components: Physical health assessment and monitoring
  • Medical Professionals: Specialized doctors and healthcare providers
  • Treatment Settings: Established clinical environments
  • Procedures: Evidence-based medical protocols
  • Best Practices: Continuous evaluation and adjustment of treatment plans

Psychiatric Care and Attention

Addressing underlying mental health issues that contribute to addiction through specialized psychiatric intervention.

  • Behavioral Therapies: Cognitive behavioral approaches
  • Medications: Appropriate pharmaceutical interventions
  • Support Groups: Peer support and group therapy sessions
  • Psychiatric Conditions: Treatment of co-occurring mental disorders
  • Therapeutic Approaches: Individual and group counseling

Psychological Care and Attention

Supporting individuals struggling with drug addiction by addressing underlying emotional, cognitive, and behavioral issues.

  • Key Components: Individual therapy, family counseling, trauma processing
  • Therapeutic Goals: Emotional healing and behavioral change
  • Benefits: Improved coping mechanisms and life skills
  • Challenges: Addressing resistance and maintaining engagement
  • Best Practices: Evidence-based psychological interventions

Morals and Values Education

Providing moral education that has a significant effect on health management based on ethical values and jurisprudence.

  • Impact on Recovery: Understanding how addiction affects moral compass
  • Role in Recovery: Rebuilding personal values and ethics
  • Behavioral Change: Developing new moral frameworks
  • Best Practices: Integration of spiritual and ethical guidance
  • Community Values: Reconnecting with societal moral standards

If you or someone you know needs support in their recovery journey, or if you want to contribute to our cause, we are here to help.
