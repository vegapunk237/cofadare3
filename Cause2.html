Rehabilitation Phase

Socio-economic integration of drug addicts is a complex process that involves various mechanisms to support individuals in overcoming addiction and reintegrating into society.

Rebuilding Lives, Restoring Hope

Our rehabilitation program goes beyond recovery. We focus on comprehensive socio-economic integration, providing the tools, support, and opportunities necessary for individuals to rebuild their lives and become productive members of society once again.

Holistic Recovery

Comprehensive approach addressing physical, mental, and social well-being

Economic Integration

Vocational training and employment opportunities for sustainable independence

Social Reintegration

Reconnecting individuals with families, communities, and society

Socio-Economic Integration Mechanisms

Our multi-faceted approach ensures that every individual receives personalized support tailored to their unique needs and circumstances.

Vocational Training & Skill Development

Empowering individuals with marketable skills for economic independence and self-sufficiency.

  • Professional certification programs in various trades
  • Computer literacy and digital skills training
  • Entrepreneurship development and business skills
  • Apprenticeship programs with local businesses
  • Continuous skill upgrading and mentorship

Psychological & Social Support

Addressing mental health needs and rebuilding social connections for lasting recovery.

  • Individual and group counseling sessions
  • Peer support groups and recovery communities
  • Family therapy and relationship rebuilding
  • Life skills coaching and personal development
  • Relapse prevention strategies and coping mechanisms

Housing & Transitional Support

Providing safe, stable living environments during the critical transition period.

  • Transitional housing facilities with supervision
  • Assistance with permanent housing placement
  • Life skills training for independent living
  • Financial literacy and budgeting education
  • Community integration support services

Employment Placement & Follow-up

Connecting recovered individuals with meaningful employment opportunities and ongoing support.

  • Job placement services and career counseling
  • Resume writing and interview preparation
  • Partnerships with employers for job opportunities
  • Workplace support and advocacy
  • Long-term follow-up and career advancement support

Legal & Administrative Support

Assisting with legal matters and administrative procedures for full social reintegration.

  • Legal counseling and representation services
  • Assistance with identity documentation
  • Support for clearing legal records where applicable
  • Access to social services and benefits
  • Advocacy for rights and fair treatment

Community Engagement Programs

Fostering community acceptance and creating opportunities for meaningful participation.

  • Community service and volunteer opportunities
  • Cultural and recreational activities
  • Sports programs and wellness activities
  • Community awareness and stigma reduction campaigns
  • Peer mentorship for new program participants

Our Rehabilitation Process

A structured, step-by-step approach to ensure successful and sustainable recovery.

01

Assessment & Planning

Comprehensive evaluation of individual needs, strengths, and challenges to create personalized rehabilitation plans.

02

Active Rehabilitation

Intensive skill development, counseling, and support services to build foundations for independent living.

03

Transition & Integration

Gradual transition into community life with ongoing support, employment placement, and monitoring.

04

Follow-up & Monitoring

Continuous support and monitoring to prevent relapse and ensure sustained progress in recovery journey.

05

Independence & Success

Achieving full social and economic independence with the skills and support to maintain long-term recovery.

06

Community Leadership

Opportunities to give back through peer mentorship and community advocacy, inspiring others on their recovery journey.

Comprehensive Support Services

We provide a complete ecosystem of support to ensure every individual has the resources they need to succeed.

Medical Care

Ongoing health monitoring and treatment for physical and mental health conditions

Financial Assistance

Emergency financial support and microfinance opportunities for entrepreneurship

Education Support

Access to formal education, literacy programs, and continuing education opportunities

24/7 Hotline

Round-the-clock crisis support and counseling services for emergency situations
