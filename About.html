Who We Are

COFADARE - the Commission for Fight Against Drug Abuse and Rehabilitation is an International non-governmental Organization which main mission is to fight against drugs and non-substances abuse and rehabilitation of Drugs victims back into the society. Presently hosted in more than 20 countries in the world.

These movement is of a global initiative and action promoted by a group of experts from diverse fields; Health, Education, Transport, Civil society, Sport etc. Today, this initiative continuely integrated many societies, other institutions and government corporations to fight against this pandemic with references to the SDG of Art No: 03 Good health and well-being. They concept to fight against drugs abuse and non-substances abuse is a sustainable three dimensional action which incorporate a sustainable mechanism to prevent and suppress substances and non substances abuse which has highly scope an impact of sphere’s for the past centuries with more than 1billion vulnerable persons.

Our Mission

  • Prevention: Education and raise awareness about the risks and consequences of drugs abuse
  • Treatment: Provide accessible, evidence-based treatment and rehabilitation services for individuals struggling with substances use disorder
  • Support: Offer support and resources for individual, families, and communities affected by drug abuse
  • Rehabilitation: Empower individuals recover and reintegrate into society through holistic rehabilitation programs

Our Vision

A world free from drug abuse, where individuals and communities thrive, and those affected by substance use disorders receive comprehensive support and care.

We envision a society where prevention, treatment, and rehabilitation work together seamlessly to create lasting positive change in the lives of individuals and communities worldwide.
